# 07. Agent Brief

**Prepared:** March 2026  
**Use this first if you are an internal AI agent.**

## One-Sentence Explanation

Dominate is evolving into the premium ConversionOS tier for contractors who want to **keep the feel of their current website while moving onto NorBot's shared AI-native platform**.

## What This Offer Is

- a high-fidelity migration / rebuild onto ConversionOS
- same-brand, same-domain where feasible
- shared platform core underneath
- bounded premium delivery, not unlimited custom work

## What This Offer Is Not

- not raw source-code copying
- not a promise to reproduce every plugin/CMS behaviour
- not a requirement to transfer the domain to NorBot
- not a promise of unlimited custom website work under standard Dominate pricing

## Approved Claims

- "We can usually preserve the feel and flow of your current site."
- "We normally keep your current domain."
- "We rebuild the public-site experience on top of ConversionOS."
- "The AI features stay native to the platform, not bolted on as random widgets."
- "We confirm domain control and asset rights before migration."
- "Dominate is the premium tier for this type of work."

## Forbidden Claims

- "We can just copy your website."
- "You automatically own all of the old code."
- "We need you to transfer the domain to us."
- "Anything can be recreated inside standard Dominate pricing."
- "The custom-shell architecture is already fully implemented."
- "There is no legal complexity as long as you are the business owner."

## 30-Second Explanation to a Contractor

> "Dominate is for contractors who like the website they already have but want it to work harder. Instead of forcing you into a generic redesign, we rebuild the feel of your site on ConversionOS so you can keep the familiarity and add AI visualizer, chat, quoting, admin, and better conversion flow underneath."

## Discovery Questions

Ask these before you sound certain:

1. Do you want to keep the feel of your current site, or are you open to a full redesign?
2. Do you control the domain, or do you know who does?
3. Who built your current site?
4. What parts of the current site matter most to preserve?
5. What is missing from the site operationally today?
6. Do you have rights to the main images, logo, and copy?
7. How many pages, services, projects, and blog/resources sections matter at launch?

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Ferdie or a deeper internal review if:

- domain ownership/control is unclear
- asset rights are unclear
- the site appears much larger than a normal contractor marketing site
- the buyer expects exact code reuse
- the buyer expects unlimited custom work under standard Dominate
- the deal sounds more like business automation than website/platform migration

## Safe Language for Legal/Technical Questions

- "We usually do a clean rebuild rather than depend on the old codebase."
- "Registrar transfer is optional; DNS cutover is usually enough."
- "We would confirm rights to content and assets before migration."
- "This is general information, not legal advice."
