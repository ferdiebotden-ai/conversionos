# 03. Dominate Offer

**Prepared:** March 2026

## Working Positioning

Dominate is the premium ConversionOS offer for established contractors who want:

- city exclusivity
- the full AI-native platform
- and now, by default, a **high-fidelity rebuild of their current website feel onto ConversionOS**

This is not a generic custom website service.

The sales framing should be:

> "We keep the feel of the site you like, keep your domain, and upgrade the brain behind it."

## Dominate Promise

Under this business-shift model, Dominate promises:

- high-fidelity migration of the contractor's current public website experience into a defined delivery envelope
- same-brand, same-domain experience wherever feasible
- native ConversionOS visualizer, receptionist/chat, quote flow, admin, analytics, and premium support
- city exclusivity and founder-led onboarding

## Dominate Commercial Baseline

Use the current market-facing stack in this package:

- **Activation:** $20,000
- **Monthly:** $2,500
- **Guarantee:** 14-day confidence guarantee
- **Commercial positioning:** premium moat tier with exclusivity, platform depth, and preserved brand continuity

## Included by Default

Dominate includes the full ConversionOS platform plus a bounded high-fidelity migration envelope.

### Platform side

- AI Design Studio / visualizer
- Emma chat / receptionist experience
- lead capture and contextual intake
- quote engine and admin dashboard
- invoicing and analytics where currently supported by the platform
- founder-led onboarding and premium support

### Site-preservation side

- public-site information architecture modelled on the contractor's current site
- public visual language aligned to the contractor's current site
- same-domain cutover plan
- conversion-focused improvements to mobile behaviour, responsiveness, speed, and CTA clarity
- migration of approved copy, imagery, and public-site structure into the new build

## Standard Dominate Migration Envelope

This envelope exists to prevent Dominate from turning into unlimited agency scope.

### Included envelope

- Up to **15 key public pages or templates**
- One primary public navigation with up to **2 levels** of dropdown depth
- Standard contractor marketing interactions:
  - sticky header
  - utility/contact bar
  - hero CTA area or hero form
  - accordions / FAQ
  - galleries / lightboxes / sliders
  - testimonials / reviews sections
  - service cards and service detail templates
  - project galleries and case-study style pages
  - blog/resources index plus **up to 10 priority evergreen articles** migrated or rebuilt
  - standard scroll reveals and tasteful motion
- Up to **20 featured portfolio items** migrated as part of the initial launch scope
- Contact / consultation flow rebuilt onto ConversionOS forms and lead capture
- DNS cutover support and post-launch stabilization

### Not included by default

- full WordPress admin parity
- plugin-for-plugin recreation
- ecommerce
- membership or account portals for homeowners
- multilingual site delivery
- arbitrary CMS reproduction
- unlimited blog archive migration
- custom calculators outside existing ConversionOS capability
- third-party customer portals
- unrestricted rounds of bespoke motion/design iteration

## Scope Triggers

The following trigger a scoped addendum or Black Label review:

- more than 15 key pages/templates
- more than 10 priority blog/resource articles required at launch
- multilingual requirements
- franchise / multi-brand / multi-domain complexity
- complex scheduling or booking system replacement
- external ERP / PM / accounting integrations beyond the normal platform path
- highly custom animations or experiences that do not align with ConversionOS patterns
- demands for old-CMS editing parity rather than migration to the new operating model

## Qualification Rules

### Good Dominate candidate

- likes current site but wants more conversion power
- has credible brand presence already
- can provide domain and DNS access or decision-making authority
- can confirm rights to logos, copy, images, and testimonials
- sees the website as a revenue asset, not just an online brochure
- accepts bounded premium implementation, not endless custom work

### Disqualify or pause

- unclear domain ownership
- no control over registrar or DNS and no path to obtain it
- disputed ownership of current content or assets
- expectation that NorBot will simply "copy the current site code"
- expectation of unlimited website custom work inside the standard Dominate fee

## Sales Language

### Approved language

- "high-fidelity migration onto ConversionOS"
- "rebuild the feel and flow of your current site"
- "keep your domain and upgrade the conversion engine underneath"
- "move from static brochure site to AI-native lead-to-quote system"
- "preserve familiarity while improving responsiveness, mobile performance, and conversion flow"

### Do not use

- "copy your website"
- "clone your code"
- "we will just duplicate whatever your current developer built"
- "we can recreate anything at the same price"
- "we need you to transfer your domain to us"

## Common Objections

### "We just built our website."

Recommended answer:

> "That is exactly why this version of Dominate exists. We are not asking you to throw away the site you just paid for. We rebuild the feel of it on top of ConversionOS so you keep the familiarity and add the tools your current site does not have."

### "Do I have to change my domain?"

Recommended answer:

> "Usually no. The goal is to keep your current domain. We handle the technical cutover by pointing the domain to the new site. Registrar transfer is usually optional, not required."

### "Do I own my current website?"

Recommended answer:

> "You likely own some parts and may only have rights to use other parts. We confirm that before migration. In practice, we usually rebuild cleanly using the assets and content you are entitled to use."

## Dominate vs Black Label Boundary

### Dominate

- premium contractor website migration onto shared core
- bounded page and interaction envelope
- contractor-focused recurring platform

### Black Label

- broader business automation
- highly custom workflows
- complex systems integration
- unusually bespoke build scope
- cross-vertical or non-standard engagements

## Offer Conclusion

Dominate should be presented as:

- the premium contractor tier
- the "preserve and upgrade" tier
- the option for contractors who want less visual disruption and more platform value

It should **not** be presented as unlimited bespoke website work under a flat fee.
