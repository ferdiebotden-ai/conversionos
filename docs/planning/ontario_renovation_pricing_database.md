## 3. Permit Fees by Ontario Municipality

> **Note:** Most Ontario municipalities calculate residential renovation permit fees either as a flat rate, by square footage of work area, or as a percentage of construction value. Minor cosmetic renovations (painting, flooring replacement, cabinet refacing) typically do NOT require permits. Permits are required for structural changes, plumbing, electrical, additions, and basement finishing.

## 6. HST Rules for Residential Renovation (Ontario)

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Standard HST rate** | 13% (5% federal GST + 8% provincial PST) |
| **HST applies to** | All renovation labour and materials |
| **HST rebate (new housing)** | Available for substantial renovations (90%+ of interior removed) — up to 36% of federal portion |
| **No HST on** | Resale of used building materials (rare), some exempt financial services |
| **Contractor should collect** | HST on all invoiced amounts. Homeowners pay HST on full quoted price |
| **Display in quotes as** | Separate line item: Subtotal + HST = Total |

**For Marcus quotes:** Always show HST as a separate line item. Never include it in line-item pricing. Calculate on the subtotal.

## 8. Key Market Trends (2025–2026)

- **Material cost stabilization:** After significant post-2022 inflation, lumber and material costs have stabilized through 2025. Tariff impacts on US-imported materials (steel, fixtures) remain a factor — monitor quarterly.
- **Labour shortage:** Skilled trades remain in high demand across Ontario. Electricians, plumbers, and tilers are particularly constrained, supporting premium pricing.
- **Heat pump adoption:** Growing consumer interest in heat pumps is increasing HVAC project values. Government rebates (up to $10,600) make this an attractive upsell for contractors.
- **Basement suites trending:** Ontario's housing crisis is driving demand for legal basement apartments. This increases average basement renovation budgets ($70K–$120K with full suite) and requires more comprehensive permits.
- **LVP dominance:** Luxury vinyl plank continues to dominate flooring choices for renovations due to cost, durability, and ease of installation. Hardwood remains premium tier.

## 9. Quote Construction Guide for Marcus

### When generating a ballpark estimate, follow this structure:

```
1. PROJECT SUMMARY
   - Project type (kitchen, bathroom, basement, etc.)
   - Estimated scope (basic, mid-range, high-end)
   - Square footage or room dimensions (if provided)
   - Municipality (for permit fees and regional multiplier)

2. LINE ITEMS
   - Labour (broken into trades where possible)
   - Materials (broken into categories)
   - Permits (estimate based on municipality)
   - Contingency (10–15% of subtotal)

3. SUBTOTAL (before tax)

4. HST (13% of subtotal)

5. TOTAL ESTIMATED COST

6. CONFIDENCE LEVEL
   - HIGH: Homeowner provided room size, material preferences, and scope
   - MEDIUM: Homeowner provided project type and general scope
   - LOW: Minimal details — wide range estimate

7. ASSUMPTIONS
   - List all assumptions made (e.g., "assumes no structural changes")
   - Note what additional information would narrow the estimate
```

### Confidence Scoring Rules:

| Confidence | Criteria | Range Width |
|---|---|---|
| **HIGH** | Room size, material tier, scope, and location all provided | ±15% |
| **MEDIUM** | Project type and general scope provided, some details missing | ±25% |
| **LOW** | Only project type known, no specifics | ±35% |
