# Shadow Rollout

## Phase 1: Private Shadow Runs

- keep tenant-builder as the default production path
- run dominant-builder on selected dominant-tier prospects
- compare output quality, operator effort, and product integrity

## Phase 2: Measured Comparison

Replacement gates:
- at least 90 percent successful end-to-end shadow runs across a 10-target pilot
- median manual polish time at or below 30 minutes
- average brand fidelity at or above 4.5 out of 5
- zero critical regressions in lead capture, visualizer, chat, or quote flows
- mobile, accessibility, and performance checks pass for every pilot build
- no public exposure before approval

## Phase 3: Cutover Recommendation

Cutover is only recommended when the pilot clears every gate and the decision is recorded in the business-os decision log.
